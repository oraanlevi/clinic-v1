How It Works

Our process is designed to provide clear, efficient, and reliable access to private medical care, whether online or in person.

1. Choose a Service

Select the medical service that best fits your needs, including telemedicine, home or hotel visits, diagnostic testing, or preventive care.

2. Choose Location

Appointments may take place online, at your home, hotel, or other private location, depending on the selected service.

3. Medical Consultation

A licensed physician conducts the consultation, provides medical evaluation, and recommends appropriate care or follow-up.

4. Follow-Up & Documentation

When applicable, prescriptions, medical certificates, test coordination, or follow-up care instructions are provided.
