Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about hotel/home visits, telemedicine, and private care in Florence and Milan.

Do you provide hotel and home doctor visits in Florence and Milan?

Yes. We can arrange private medical visits directly at hotels, homes, and accommodations in Florence and Milan, depending on availability and urgency.

Are the doctors English-speaking?

Yes. Our team includes English-speaking doctors and multilingual support. Other languages (Italian/French/Spanish) may be available depending on scheduling.

How fast can you arrange a visit?

It depends on the area and urgency. In many cases we can confirm quickly by phone or WhatsApp and arrange same-day assistance.

Can I use telemedicine instead of an in-person visit?

Yes. We offer secure telemedicine consultations when appropriate. If needed, we can also arrange an in-person visit.

Do you provide documentation for insurance?

We can provide receipts and medical documentation for travel/health insurance when applicable, depending on the service provided.

How do I book?

You can book online through the form or contact us directly for immediate help. We’ll guide you and confirm availability.
