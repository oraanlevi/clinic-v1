What Is Telemedicine?

Telemedicine allows you to consult with a licensed physician remotely using secure video or phone communication. It offers convenient access to medical care without the need to visit the clinic in person.

How Online Consultations Work

Step 1: Request an appointment through our contact options.
Step 2: Connect with a doctor via secure video consultation.
Step 3: Receive medical advice, prescriptions, or follow-up instructions.

When Telemedicine Is Recommended

Telemedicine is suitable for non-emergency consultations, follow-up visits, prescription renewals, general medical advice, and ongoing care discussions.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Online consultations are conducted using secure, privacy-conscious systems designed to protect patient confidentiality and personal medical information.
